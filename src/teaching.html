Workshop: Quantum Fractals with Black Girls Code

I organized and led a quantum computing workshop in partnership with Black Girls Code and the LA program director. Our team of 10 volunteers guided students through hands-on activities exploring quantum superposition, entanglement, and circuit building on IBM’s Quantum Composer. The students visualized quantum math through fractal patterns and shared their work via collaborative FigJam presentations. More information about the event is available here.
