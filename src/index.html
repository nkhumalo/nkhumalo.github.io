Hello! Ciao! Mhoroi!! Yebo!! Bom dia!!! I am a chemistry Ph.D student in the Narang Lab at UCLA. My research interests range from quantum information for quantum chemistry simulation, machine learning for protein active site prediction, and performance optimization for HPC applications.

In 2024, I served as a Fulbright research grantee in Italy at the University of Padua, completing a project entitled, "An Investigation of Selenium-based Compounds that Inhibit SARS-CoV-2."

I graduated with a B.A. in Computer Science and Italian with a minor in Chemistry from Bowdoin College in 2023. I completed a natural language processing senior thesis entitled, "Una Comprensione Computazionale della Psiche Emotiva e Ordine nelle Ballate del Decameron: Stilometria e Elaborazione del Linguaggio Naturale" in the Italian department under the supervision of Dr. Crystal Hall. As an undergraduate, I completed computational chemistry research projects at Bowdoin College, Northeastern University, and Brookhaven National Laboratory, working under Dr. Allison Dzubak, Dr. Mary Jo Ondrechen, and Dr. Hubertus Van Dam, respectively.
